Lata Mangeshkar sang her first film song in 1942 at the age of thirteen. She continued recording for over seventy years. In that time she sang for virtually every major actress in Hindi cinema and worked with every major music director of the golden era — from S.D. Burman and Madan Mohan to R.D. Burman and A.R. Rahman.

Her voice was technically precise but never cold. It carried something that resists description — a quality that made listeners feel seen.
